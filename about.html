Who Is Nitin Gadura?

Nitin Gadura was born and raised in Ozone Park, Queens — a neighborhood where South Asian, Caribbean, Punjabi, and Guyanese families have built their lives for generations. This isn't a market he studied in a classroom. It's the block he grew up on, the block he still serves, and the streets he knows by name.

His father, Vinod K. Gadura, founded Gadura Real Estate LLC in 2006, making the family brokerage one of the longest-standing South Asian-owned real estate firms in Queens. Nitin grew up watching his father handle transactions, negotiate on behalf of families, and earn the community's trust deal by deal. That experience shaped everything about how Nitin approaches real estate today — honest advice over commissions, relationships over transactions.

What sets Nitin apart from most licensed agents in Queens is language. He speaks English, Hindi, and Punjabi fluently — which means if you are a Hindi-speaking or Punjabi-speaking buyer or seller, you do not need an interpreter. You get your real estate agent directly. Complex contract terms, disclosure documents, mortgage explanations — all of it, in your language, at your pace.

He serves buyers, sellers, and investors across Queens, Brooklyn, Nassau County (Long Island), and Manhattan. His client base spans first-time homebuyers navigating co-op boards to multi-family investors looking at cap rates in East New York. Whether you're selling a two-family in Richmond Hill or purchasing your first condo in Howard Beach, Nitin has handled it.

His philosophy is simple: answer the phone, tell the truth, and explain every step. Real estate is the largest financial transaction most people will ever make. Nitin treats it that way — 7 days a week, no voicemail runaround, no high-pressure tactics. Just honest guidance from someone who knows Queens the way very few agents do.
