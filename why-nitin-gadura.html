The Six Things I Actually Do for Clients

1. Hyper-Local Pricing

I don't price from ZIP averages. I pull block-level OneKey® MLS sold comps, absorb condition differences, and quote a real range. For Howard Beach, Richmond Hill, Ozone Park, and Rockaway I know which streets carry premiums and which don't.

2. Negotiation on Every Term

Price is only one of twelve terms that move money in a deal. Contingencies, repair credits, seller concessions, appraisal gap language, closing date, rent-back, personal property — I negotiate all of them, not just the headline number.

3. Multilingual Representation

Queens is the most linguistically diverse county in America. My team reaches buyers and sellers in English, Hindi, Punjabi, Bengali, Urdu, and Spanish. If you're selling a Richmond Hill or South Richmond Hill home, this meaningfully widens your buyer pool.

4. Attorney & Lender Network

Every closing involves your NY-licensed real estate attorney, your lender, title company, and inspector. I coordinate all of them on one timeline. I also refer SONYMA- and HomeFirst-participating lenders for first-time buyers who need down-payment assistance.

5. Off-Market Inventory

Not every home sells on the MLS. I work pocket listings through my Queens investor and family-owned-home network — especially for 2-family and 3-family buyers priced out of the public market.

6. Transparent Commissions

Commissions in NY are negotiable and not set by law. I put mine in writing before you list, explain every cost, and never surprise you at closing. Under the 2024 NAR settlement, buyer-broker agreements are required before MLS tours — mine are plain-English and fully negotiable.

Who I Work With

First-Time Buyers

I walk first-time buyers through pre-approval, down-payment assistance (SONYMA and HomeFirst stacking), FHA 2-family math, closing cost projection, and offer strategy. Many of my first-time buyers close with under $15,000 out of pocket — program stacking makes that possible.

Multi-Family Investors

South Ozone Park, Richmond Hill, South Richmond Hill, Jamaica, and Queens Village are the densest 2- and 3-family submarkets in NYC. I find off-market opportunities, run rent-roll and cap-rate analysis, and coordinate 1031 exchange replacement searches within the 45-day window.

Sellers — Including Divorce, Estate, Senior Downsizing

I list homes with pricing backed by MLS data, not emotion. For divorce sales I maintain neutral communication with both spouses and both attorneys. For estate sales I coordinate with probate counsel and honor the family's pace. For senior downsizings I bring patience, vendor referrals, and no pressure.

South Asian & Indo-Caribbean Families

My roots and my team's languages serve the Punjabi, Sikh, Hindu, Bengali, Pakistani, Guyanese, and Trinidadian communities that are the backbone of South Queens. Multigenerational homes, 2-family owner-occupied arrangements, and culturally-aware marketing are specialties.
