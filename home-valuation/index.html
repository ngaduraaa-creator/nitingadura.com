Home Valuation FAQ

How much is my home worth in Queens?

Home values vary by neighborhood. The median across Queens is ~$650K in 2026, ranging from $450K in some areas to over $1.5M in Jamaica Estates. I provide valuations based on real comparable sales in your specific neighborhood — submit the form above for yours.

Is this really free?

Yes — completely free, no strings attached. A comparative market analysis is a standard service that real estate agents provide to homeowners considering selling. There's no obligation to list with me.

Can I get my valuation in Hindi or Punjabi?

Absolutely. I speak fluent Hindi, Punjabi, and English. I'll walk you through every number in whatever language you're most comfortable with.

How is this different from Zillow's Zestimate?

Zillow uses an algorithm that can be off by 5-15% in Queens because it can't account for property condition, renovations, or hyperlocal factors. My valuation uses actual MLS sales data, personal knowledge of your neighborhood, and adjustments for your home's specific features.
