Programs Available to Woodhaven First-Time Buyers

HPD HomeFirst — Up to $100,000

  • Income limit: 80% of Area Median Income (~$107K for family of 4)
  • Must complete 8+ hours of homebuyer education
  • Property must be your primary residence in NYC
  • Applies to 1–4 family homes, condos, co-ops

SONYMA — Below-Market Rate Mortgages

  • State of New York Mortgage Agency: below-market fixed rates for first-timers
  • Down payment as low as 3%
  • Queens income limit: ~$175K (1–2 persons)
  • SONYMA Program Details

FHA Loans — 3.5% Down

  • Down payment of only $27,300 on a $780,000 home
  • 580+ credit score required
  • Works for 1–4 family where you live in one unit

The Owner-Occupant 2-Family Strategy

Buy a 2-family home in Woodhaven, live in one unit, rent the other. FHA allows this with 3.5% down. Your tenant's rent — estimated $2,900/month in Woodhaven — helps pay your mortgage. This is the single most powerful wealth-building tool I recommend to first-time buyers in this market.
