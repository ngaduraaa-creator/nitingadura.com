My Take on Howard Beach

I've worked with buyers and sellers throughout Howard Beach for years — and I can tell you firsthand: howard beach is queens' hidden waterfront gem — cape cods and split-levels with canal access or jamaica bay views at prices below comparable nassau county waterfront communities.

What most agents won't tell you: Howard Beach is one of only a handful of NYC neighborhoods where residential streets end at Jamaica Bay — the 25-square-mile estuary that forms the southern edge of Queens.
