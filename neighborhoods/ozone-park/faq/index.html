What is the average home price in Ozone Park, Queens?
The median home price in Ozone Park is $755,000 as of 2026-04, up +4.9% year-over-year, per Zillow / Redfin.
Is Ozone Park a good neighborhood to buy in?
The A-train terminus at Ozone Park–Lefferts Blvd puts Midtown Manhattan just 40 minutes away. With median home prices below Forest Hills and Jackson Heights, this is one of Queens' best value propositions. With a 40-minute subway commute to Manhattan and strong appreciation history, Ozone Park offers excellent long-term value.

What is Ozone Park known for?
Ozone Park is home to one of the largest Guyanese-American communities in the United States, with Liberty Avenue serving as a cultural and culinary hub for the diaspora.
Are there first-time buyer programs available in Ozone Park?
Yes. NYC HPD HomeFirst provides up to $100,000 in down payment assistance for income-eligible first-time buyers. SONYMA offers below-market rates. FHA allows 3.5% down. I can walk you through which programs you qualify for.

Can I invest in a 2-family home in Ozone Park?
Yes — 2-family homes make up 50% of Ozone Park's housing stock. Combined rental income runs approximately $5,900/month. The owner-occupant strategy (live in one unit, rent the other with FHA financing) is particularly powerful here.
