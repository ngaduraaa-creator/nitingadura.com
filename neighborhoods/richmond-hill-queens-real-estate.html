My Take on Richmond Hill

I've worked with buyers and sellers throughout Richmond Hill for years — and I can tell you firsthand: richmond hill offers well-preserved victorian architecture, forest park access, and a vibrant liberty avenue corridor — all at prices still below the queens borough median.

What most agents won't tell you: Richmond Hill's Liberty Avenue is home to one of the largest concentrations of Indo-Caribbean and Punjabi businesses outside of South Asia — including grocery stores, temples, restaurants, and professional services serving dozens of communities.
