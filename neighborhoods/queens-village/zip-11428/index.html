An Insider's Look at Queens Village (ZIP 11428)

Block-level perspective from a broker who actively works this ZIP — not boilerplate.

Submarket Breakdown

ZIP 11428 covers central Queens Village. The blocks north of Hempstead Avenue have the most 1-family detached homes on standard lots; south of Hempstead Avenue attached row homes and small 2-family appear more frequently; blocks closer to the Cross Island Parkway on the eastern edge trend toward slightly larger lots.

Housing Stock & Typical Price Bands

Detached 1-family homes run $700K–$900K; attached row homes run $625K–$775K; 2-family brick (when available) run $850K–$1.1M; semi-attached homes with driveways run $700K–$850K. Lot sizes are slightly smaller than in adjacent 11427.

Who This ZIP Fits Best

Best fit for: Black, Caribbean (Guyanese, Jamaican, Trinidadian, Haitian), and Indo-Caribbean families with deep neighborhood roots; first-time buyers using FHA on small 2-family homes; LIRR commuters working in Manhattan or Hicksville/Garden City.

Honest Tradeoffs I Tell My Clients

Honest tradeoffs: parking pressure in denser pockets; school-district variation by block — verify zoning before offer; some 2-family conversions may have illegal basement units requiring DOB compliance review; ongoing investment cycle as long-tenured owners sell to younger families.
