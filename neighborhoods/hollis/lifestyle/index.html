Living in Hollis, Queens — My Honest Lifestyle Guide

By Nitin Gadura — who actually knows this neighborhood · Updated 2026-04-20

Most neighborhood guides are written by people who've never spent a weekend in the place they're describing. I've worked in Hollis for years. Here's what it's actually like to live here.

The Character of Hollis

Hollis leads SE Queens in single-family home concentration — 43% of properties are detached 1-family homes on tree-lined blocks near the Nassau border.

Hollis holds a special place in American music history — the neighborhood is the birthplace of hip-hop legends Run-DMC, LL Cool J, and Ja Rule, all of whom grew up on Hollis Avenue.

Food & Culture

Hollis reflects Queens' extraordinary cultural diversity in its restaurants, grocery stores, and community life. This is one of the most culinarily diverse counties in the United States — your neighbors are likely to represent dozens of countries and culinary traditions. The main commercial corridors have everything from halal butchers and South Asian grocers to Dominican bakeries and Italian-American delis.

Community Feel

Median household income of $80,870 and a population of 18,486 creates the critical mass for a full range of neighborhood services: libraries, community gardens, youth sports leagues, houses of worship, and block associations. Hollis residents tend to stay — that longevity creates genuine community bonds.

Weekend in Hollis

  • Cunningham Park (southern access) — walking, cycling, picnics
  • Jamaica Avenue or main corridor shopping and dining
  • Quick trip to Rockaway Beach via A train (seasonal)
  • Aqueduct Racetrack (seasonal thoroughbred racing)
  • Jamaica Bay Wildlife Refuge — a national park minutes away

My Take

Hollis is the kind of neighborhood where you know your neighbors. Where kids play outside. Where the local diner has been open since 1975. If you're looking for Manhattan-style nightlife, this isn't it. If you're looking for a real home in a real neighborhood with strong community ties — Hollis delivers.
