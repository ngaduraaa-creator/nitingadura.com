Hollis Real Estate FAQ — Nitin Gadura Answers

Common questions I hear from buyers, sellers, and families considering Hollis

What is the average home price in Hollis, Queens?
The median home price in Hollis is $748,000 as of 2026-04, up +9.0% year-over-year, per Homes.com / Rocket Homes.
Is Hollis a good neighborhood to buy in?
Hollis offers more single-family homes per block than any other SE Queens neighborhood — 43% detached 1-family homes with LIRR access and Nassau County borders. With a 38-minute subway commute to Manhattan and strong appreciation history, Hollis offers excellent long-term value.

What is Hollis known for?
Hollis holds a special place in American music history — the neighborhood is the birthplace of hip-hop legends Run-DMC, LL Cool J, and Ja Rule, all of whom grew up on Hollis Avenue.
Are there first-time buyer programs available in Hollis?
Yes. NYC HPD HomeFirst provides up to $100,000 in down payment assistance for income-eligible first-time buyers. SONYMA offers below-market rates. FHA allows 3.5% down. I can walk you through which programs you qualify for.

Can I invest in a 2-family home in Hollis?
Yes — 2-family homes make up 35% of Hollis's housing stock. Combined rental income runs approximately $5,400/month. The owner-occupant strategy (live in one unit, rent the other with FHA financing) is particularly powerful here.
