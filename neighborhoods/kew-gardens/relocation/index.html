Why Families Move to Kew Gardens

Kew Gardens' Tudor Revival architecture, PS 99's 8/10 GreatSchools rating, and E/F express access make it Queens' top choice for professionals and families.

PS 99 Kew Gardens earns an 8/10 GreatSchools rating with 76% math proficiency — making it one of the highest-rated elementary schools in all of SE Queens.

Median household income in Kew Gardens: $88,275 · Population: 24,052 · Commute to Manhattan: 28 min.

Practical Relocation Checklist

  1. USPS change of address (usps.com)
  2. NY DMV address update within 10 days (required by law)
  3. ConEd + National Grid utilities setup (call 2 weeks before move-in)
  4. Voter registration update (elections.ny.gov)
  5. School registration at district office (bring proof of address)
  6. Queens Public Library card (free, invaluable)
