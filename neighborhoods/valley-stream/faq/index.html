The median home price in Valley Stream is $750,000 as of 2026-04, up +6.0% year-over-year, per Redfin / PropertyShark / Zillow.
Is Valley Stream a good neighborhood to buy in?
Valley Stream offers classic Nassau County suburban living — Cape Cod and Colonial homes, Valley Stream State Park, Green Acres Mall — with four LIRR branches providing unmatched commuter access. With a 36-minute subway commute to Manhattan and strong appreciation history, Valley Stream offers excellent long-term value.

What is Valley Stream known for?
Valley Stream State Park, covering 190 acres along Brookside Drive, is one of the few state parks in Nassau County — offering hiking trails, ballfields, and picnic areas minutes from residential streets.
Are there first-time buyer programs available in Valley Stream?
Yes. NYC HPD HomeFirst provides up to $100,000 in down payment assistance for income-eligible first-time buyers. SONYMA offers below-market rates. FHA allows 3.5% down. I can walk you through which programs you qualify for.

Can I invest in a 2-family home in Valley Stream?
Yes — 2-family homes make up 26% of Valley Stream's housing stock. Combined rental income runs approximately $5,900/month. The owner-occupant strategy (live in one unit, rent the other with FHA financing) is particularly powerful here.
