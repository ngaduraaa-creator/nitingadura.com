How I Explain Valley Stream's Taxes to My Clients

Property taxes in Valley Stream are calculated differently depending on whether you're in Nassau County (uses assessed value × tax rate). For a median-priced home in Valley Stream at $750,000, you're looking at roughly $10,546/year — about $878/month in your escrow payment.

Exemptions I Always Tell My Clients About

  • STAR Exemption: Basic STAR saves $300–$1,500/year for owner-occupants. Enhanced STAR for seniors 65+ saves even more. Apply at NYS Department of Taxation.
  • Veterans Exemption: Eligible veterans receive an assessed value reduction
  • Senior Citizen Exemption: Income-qualified seniors 65+ may get up to 50% reduction
  • Disabled Persons Exemption: For qualifying homeowners with disabilities

My advice: Apply for every exemption you qualify for on day one. Most clients leave $300–$1,200/year on the table by not applying.

Appealing Your Assessment

In Nassau County, homeowners can file with the Assessment Review Commission (ARC) by March 1 each year. Many Nassau homeowners hire tax grievance firms that work on contingency — no reduction, no fee. In my experience, a well-documented appeal in Nassau often succeeds.
