An Insider's Look at South Jamaica (ZIP 11433)

Block-level perspective from a broker who actively works this ZIP — not boilerplate.

Submarket Breakdown

ZIP 11433 covers South Jamaica. The blocks near Sutphin Boulevard are denser with attached row homes and 2-family brick; central South Jamaica has 1-family detached and semi-attached homes; the blocks near 165th Street on the east side have a mix of 1-family detached and semi-attached homes on slightly larger lots.

Housing Stock & Typical Price Bands

1-family detached homes typically run $625K–$800K; attached row homes run $550K–$700K; 2-family brick are common at $775K–$1.05M and serve as the primary investor and house-hacker product; semi-attached homes with driveways run $650K–$825K.

Who This ZIP Fits Best

Best fit for: Caribbean (Guyanese, Jamaican, Trinidadian), Black professional, and Indo-Caribbean families with multi-generational roots; FHA 2-family house-hackers entering homeownership; first-time buyers using SONYMA and HomeFirst down-payment-assistance programs; LIRR commuters using Jamaica hub.

Honest Tradeoffs I Tell My Clients

Honest tradeoffs: Sutphin Boulevard commercial corridor has heavy traffic; some blocks have ongoing investment cycle and condition variation house-to-house; verify Certificate of Occupancy on all 2-family purchases; school-district variation requires per-address verification.
