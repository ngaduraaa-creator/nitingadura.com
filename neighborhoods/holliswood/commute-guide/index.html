What My Clients Tell Me

Most of my buyer clients who work in Manhattan are pleasantly surprised by the Holliswood commute. At 40 minutes, you're spending less time in transit than many people who live in Brooklyn neighborhoods that are "closer" on a map but require more transfers. And you're paying significantly less for housing.

Driving & Parking

For residents who drive, Holliswood offers street parking and driveways — a far cry from the parking situation in northwest Queens or Manhattan. Most Holliswood homes include a driveway or garage space. Access to major Queens arteries makes driving practical for commutes to Brooklyn, Long Island, or the outer boroughs.

Airport Access

JFK International Airport is approximately 10–20 minutes from Holliswood by car — a genuine quality-of-life benefit for frequent travelers. LaGuardia is reachable in 25–35 minutes.

Transit & Your Home Value

Transit access is directly reflected in Holliswood home prices. Homes within walking distance of subway stations consistently sell faster and for more money. When I evaluate homes for buyers, transit walk time is one of the first things I assess.
