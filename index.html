About Me

Born in Queens. Built for This Community.

I'm Nitin Gadura, a Licensed NYS Real Estate Salesperson at Gadura Real Estate LLC — the family brokerage my father Vinod Gadura founded in Ozone Park back in 2006. I grew up on these streets, went to school here, and I know every neighborhood from the inside out.

As a Punjabi real estate professional, I understand the unique needs of South Asian, Indian, Guyanese, Bengali, and Caribbean families navigating one of the biggest financial decisions of their lives. I speak Hindi, Punjabi, and English fluently — and I work with clients who want an agent that truly gets their culture, values, and goals.

I don't do high-pressure sales. I don't disappear after the first showing. I answer my phone 7 days a week, I explain every step in plain language, and I fight for the best deal for my clients — every single time.
