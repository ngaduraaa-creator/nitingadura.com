Complete Compliance Summary

Gadura Real Estate LLC and Nitin Gadura operate in full compliance with all applicable federal, New York State, and New York City real estate laws, including but not limited to:

  • Federal Fair Housing Act (42 U.S.C. §3601 et seq.)
    Prohibits discrimination in the sale, rental, or financing of housing based on race, color, national origin, religion, sex, familial status, or disability.
  • NY State Human Rights Law (NY Exec Law §296)
    Extends fair housing protection to include age, creed, sexual orientation, gender identity, military status, marital status, source of income, and domestic violence victim status.
  • NYC Human Rights Law (NYC Admin Code §8-107)
    Adds lawful source of income (Section 8), immigration status, partnership status, unemployment status, caregiver status, and arrest/conviction history to protected classes.
  • NY Agency Disclosure (NY RPL §443)
    Required disclosure of agency relationships — seller's agent, buyer's agent, dual agent, broker's agent — provided before first showing or signing of any representation agreement.
  • Standard Operating Procedures (NY RPL §442-H)
    Written procedures for identification requirements, buyer representation agreements (NAR Settlement 2024), showing requirements, and offer presentation are maintained and available upon request.
  • Property Condition Disclosure Statement (NY RPL §462)
    Sellers of residential 1-4 unit property must complete and deliver a PCDS to buyers before contract signing, or provide a $500 credit at closing.
  • Lead Paint Disclosure (42 U.S.C. §4852d)
    Required for all homes built before 1978. Sellers must disclose known lead hazards, provide EPA pamphlet, and give buyers 10 days to conduct lead inspection.
  • NAR Settlement Compliance (2024)
    Effective August 17, 2024: buyer broker compensation is not included in MLS. Written buyer representation agreement required before touring any property. Compensation is fully negotiable.
  • Distressed Homeowner / Foreclosure Counseling Notice (NY RPL §265-b)
    Homeowners in distress are advised of their right to free foreclosure counseling. HUD-approved housing counselors are available at (800) 569-4287 or hud.gov.
  • ADA Website Accessibility
    Nitin Gadura is committed to maintaining a website accessible to individuals with disabilities. For accessibility assistance, contact (917) 705-0132.
  • MLS / Data Accuracy Disclaimer
    All listing data and property information is deemed reliable but not guaranteed. Buyers and sellers should independently verify all information including square footage, taxes, and school zones.
