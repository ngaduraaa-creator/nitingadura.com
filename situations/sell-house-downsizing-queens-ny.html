Common Questions

FAQ — Downsizing Your Queens Home

Should I sell my Queens home before or after buying a smaller place?

Selling first gives certainty about your budget and avoids carrying two homes, but you may need temporary housing. Buying first eliminates moving twice but requires bridge financing. In Queens, most downsizers sell first and negotiate a rent-back agreement (staying in the home 30–60 days post-closing) to allow time to close on the smaller purchase.

What should I know about buying a co-op when downsizing in Queens?

Queens has abundant co-op inventory in Forest Hills, Rego Park, Kew Gardens, and Jackson Heights. Co-op purchases require board approval — a financial package review and board interview that can take 30–90 days. You'll also review the building's financials, proprietary lease, house rules, and flip tax. I can represent you on the buy side to manage this process.

How does the $500,000 capital gains exclusion apply when I downsize?

If you've lived in your Queens home as a primary residence for 2 of the last 5 years, you can exclude up to $250,000 (single) or $500,000 (married filing jointly) in capital gains. Given Queens appreciation, many long-time homeowners have gains approaching or exceeding this limit. Consult a CPA before listing to plan accordingly.

What happens to my property taxes when I downsize in NY?

New York does not have statewide property tax portability. Your taxes on the new property are based on its assessed value. However, if you're 65+, the Senior Citizens Homeowners' Exemption (SCHE) in NYC can reduce assessed value by 5–50% based on income. Ask your attorney about eligibility when you buy.

How long does the downsizing sale process take in Queens?

The sale typically takes 60–90 days from listing to closing. If you're moving into a co-op, factor 30–90 days for board approval after signing a contract. Total timeline from listing your current home to settling into your new place: roughly 4–6 months. I'll coordinate both transactions to minimize gap and overlap.
